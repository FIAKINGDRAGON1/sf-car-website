Exterior Design

The Hyundai Sonata 2026 Sedan features a sleek, modern exterior with aerodynamic lines and a bold front fascia. LED headlights and taillights, refined body contours, and available larger alloy wheels give the Sonata a contemporary and upscale presence. The long wheelbase and low stance blend elegance with athletic character, making it visually appealing without being overly aggressive.

Engine & Performance

The 2026 Hyundai Sonata offers a range of powertrains to suit different driving needs. The standard 2.5-liter four-cylinder delivers smooth and reliable everyday performance, while the turbocharged 2.5-liter engine in the sporty N Line produces strong acceleration and dynamic power. A hybrid option combines a 2.0-liter engine with an electric motor to improve fuel economy without compromising drivability. Transitional shifts and composed handling help the Sonata balance comfort and performance on both city roads and highways.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the 2026 Sonata offers a well-appointed cabin focused on comfort and technology. Soft touch surfaces, supportive seating, and a spacious layout create a pleasant environment for both driver and passengers. Standard dual-zone automatic climate control, a large touchscreen infotainment system, and wireless smartphone integration help make every journey enjoyable. Higher trims include premium materials, digital instrument clusters, and advanced convenience features.
