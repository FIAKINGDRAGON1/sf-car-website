Exterior Design

The Nissan Rogue 2025 SUV boasts a modern, sculpted exterior with bold lines and a confident stance. Its V-Motion grille, LED headlights and taillights, and available larger alloy wheels give the Rogue a premium and athletic look. Functional roof rails, sleek body contours, and aerodynamic styling enhance both utility and visual appeal, making it a standout in its segment.

Engine & Performance

The 2025 Nissan Rogue comes with versatile powertrain options to match varying driving preferences. The standard 2.5L four-cylinder engine provides smooth, reliable performance for daily commuting. For those seeking more power, the Rogue offers a 2.5L turbocharged engine delivering responsive acceleration, while a 2.0L hybrid option balances fuel efficiency with strong performance. All configurations feature refined handling and comfortable ride quality, whether navigating city streets or cruising on the highway.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the Nissan Rogue 2025 offers a spacious and tech-forward cabin designed for comfort and convenience. Premium materials, ergonomic seating, and a thoughtful layout ensure a relaxing experience for both driver and passengers. Features include dual-zone automatic climate control, an intuitive touchscreen infotainment system with smartphone integration, and ample cargo space. Higher trims add leather upholstery, digital instrument displays, and advanced convenience technologies.
