Exterior Design

The Honda HR-V 2021 features a compact yet sporty exterior with coupe-inspired styling. Hidden rear door handles, a sculpted body profile, and a bold front fascia give it a youthful and modern appearance. Its compact dimensions make it easy to maneuver in urban environments while still offering SUV-like presence.

Engine & Performance

The 2021 Honda HR-V is powered by a reliable 1.8-liter engine designed for smooth and efficient everyday driving. While not focused on high performance, the HR-V delivers predictable acceleration and confident handling, making it well-suited for city driving and light highway use. Its elevated driving position enhances visibility and control.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the HR-V offers a practical and flexible cabin layout. Honda’s Magic Seat® system allows multiple cargo configurations, providing exceptional versatility for its class. Comfortable seating, simple controls, and good headroom ensure a pleasant experience for both daily commutes and weekend trips.
