Exterior Design

The 2025 Malibu features a sleek and balanced exterior design with clean lines and a refined profile. LED headlights, a modern grille, and sculpted body panels give the sedan a polished and contemporary appearance that appeals to drivers seeking a sophisticated yet understated look.

Engine & Performance

The Chevrolet Malibu 2025 offers a range of efficient and responsive powertrains. The standard 1.5-liter turbo engine delivers smooth acceleration and solid fuel economy for daily driving. On select trims, the 2.0-liter turbo engine boosts output for stronger performance. Well-tuned suspension and precise steering provide confident handling on highways and city roads.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the Malibu offers a comfortable and user-friendly cabin with supportive seating and quality materials. An intuitive infotainment system with touchscreen display, connectivity features, and available premium audio enhances the driving experience. Spacious front and rear seating make the Malibu well-suited for daily commuting and longer trips.
