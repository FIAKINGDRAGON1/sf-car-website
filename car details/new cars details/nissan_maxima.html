Exterior Design

The 2025 Nissan Maxima features a bold and sophisticated design that blends elegance with sportiness. Its signature V-Motion grille, sharp LED headlights, and sculpted body lines give the sedan a commanding presence on the road. Aerodynamic contours, available larger alloy wheels, and a sleek low-slung profile enhance both style and performance, creating a sedan that is athletic yet refined.

Engine & Performance

The Nissan Maxima 2025 is powered by a 3.5L V6 engine delivering smooth, high-output performance suitable for both city and highway driving. An optional hybrid variant improves fuel efficiency without sacrificing responsiveness. The Maxima combines precise handling with a comfortable, composed ride, featuring available sport-tuned suspension and advanced transmission for seamless acceleration.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the 2025 Maxima offers a premium cabin with a focus on driver engagement and passenger comfort. Leather-appointed seating, soft-touch surfaces, and a driver-centric layout provide a luxurious experience. Technology includes a large touchscreen infotainment system with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, dual-zone automatic climate control, and available premium audio systems. Higher trims add digital instrument displays, heated and ventilated seats, and advanced convenience features for a refined journey.
