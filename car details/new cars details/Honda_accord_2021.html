Exterior Design

The Honda Accord 2021 features a bold and mature exterior design with a wide stance and flowing body lines. A prominent front grille, slim LED headlights, and sculpted side panels give it a premium and athletic appearance. The overall design reflects elegance and confidence, positioning the Accord as a standout midsize sedan.

Engine & Performance

The 2021 Honda Accord delivers strong and refined performance with its turbocharged engine lineup. The 1.5-liter turbo provides smooth acceleration and excellent fuel efficiency, while the available 2.0-liter turbocharged engine offers noticeably stronger power for more dynamic driving. Precise steering and a well-tuned chassis make the Accord engaging yet comfortable on long journeys.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the Accord offers a spacious and upscale cabin with high-quality materials and thoughtful layout. Comfortable seating, a digital driver display, and a responsive infotainment system enhance the driving experience. Excellent legroom, a quiet interior, and refined ride quality make it well-suited for both daily commuting and long-distance travel.
