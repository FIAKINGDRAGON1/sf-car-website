Exterior Design

The exterior of the KIA Carnival 2025 blends SUV-inspired styling with the practicality of a multi-purpose vehicle. A bold front grille, modern LED lighting, and clean body lines give it a premium and commanding road presence. Unlike traditional minivans, the Carnival emphasizes a more upscale and contemporary design.

Engine & Performance

The 2025 KIA Carnival is powered by a refined 3.5-liter V6 engine that delivers smooth and confident performance. Designed for family comfort rather than sportiness, the engine provides strong low-end torque for effortless cruising, highway merging, and carrying passengers with ease. The ride remains stable and quiet even on long journeys.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the Carnival offers a spacious and luxurious cabin with seating for up to eight passengers. High-quality materials, wide seats, and generous legroom ensure maximum comfort for all occupants. Advanced infotainment, multiple USB ports, and configurable seating layouts make it ideal for families, long trips, and executive transport.
