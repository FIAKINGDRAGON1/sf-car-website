Exterior Design

The exterior of the Explorer 2025 features a bold and muscular design that emphasizes both utility and modern style. A prominent grille, LED headlamps, and defined body lines give it a commanding presence. Roof rails and available larger wheels enhance both its adventurous look and practical versatility.

Engine & Performance

The 2025 Ford Explorer delivers a blend of capability and comfort with a range of powerful engines. The standard 2.3-liter EcoBoost® inline-4 offers solid everyday performance and fuel efficiency, while the available 3.0-liter EcoBoost® V6 boosts power for towing and spirited driving. With responsive acceleration and composed handling, the Explorer is well-suited for both city roads and weekend adventures.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the Explorer offers a comfortable and family-oriented cabin with intuitive ergonomics. Spacious seating for up to seven passengers, generous cargo space, and quality materials contribute to a relaxed driving experience. Standard tech includes a large touchscreen infotainment system with connectivity features, while higher trims add premium touches like leather seating and advanced climate control.
