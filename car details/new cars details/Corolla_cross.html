Exterior Design

Toyota has styled the Corolla Cross 2025 to blend practicality with modern SUV cues. It features a bold front fascia, sharper LED headlights, and athletic body lines that give it a confident road presence. Elevated ground clearance and rugged design details make it versatile for urban and light off-road environments alike.

Engine & Performance

The 2025 Toyota Corolla Cross delivers a balanced mix of performance and efficiency. Its standard 2.0-liter petrol engine provides smooth acceleration and capable everyday performance. The available hybrid powertrain enhances fuel economy while maintaining responsive power delivery. The Cross offers confident highway cruising and composed handling around town.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the Corolla Cross offers a comfortable and functional cabin with supportive seats and quality materials. The layout is intuitive, with a user-friendly infotainment system and generous storage space. Rear passengers enjoy good headroom and legroom, while cargo capacity remains strong for a compact SUV.
