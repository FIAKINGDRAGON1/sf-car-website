Exterior Design

The Toyota Raize 2025 sports a modern and bold exterior that is both compact and stylish. Its upright stance, sharp LED headlamps, and prominent grille give it a rugged, youthful look while remaining practical for urban use. Sculpted body lines and available two-tone roof options enhance its visual appeal, making the Raize stand out in the subcompact SUV segment.

Engine & Performance

The 2025 Toyota Raize is equipped with a 1.2-liter turbocharged engine that balances efficiency and everyday performance. While it is not designed for high-speed athletic driving, the powertrain provides smooth acceleration suitable for city streets and suburban roads. Its light curb weight and efficient transmission contribute to confident handling with good low-end torque for quick starts and merge situations.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the Raize offers a smartly laid out cabin with a focus on practicality and comfort. Spacious seating accommodates passengers comfortably despite its compact footprint, and thoughtful storage solutions make the cabin versatile for daily tasks. A user-friendly infotainment system with touchscreen display and smartphone integration ensures connectivity on the go.

  • Lane Departure Alert
  • Adaptive Cruise Control (available on select trims)
  • Multiple Airbags and Stability Control
