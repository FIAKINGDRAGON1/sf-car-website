Exterior Design

The Elantra 2025 features a modern and bold exterior design with sharp lines and distinctive geometric elements. LED headlights and a sporty front grille create a striking visual presence, while aerodynamic body contours improve efficiency and style. The overall design balances youthful energy with refined sophistication.

Engine & Performance

The 2025 Hyundai Elantra offers a range of refined powertrains to match different driving preferences. The standard 2.0-liter engine delivers smooth and efficient daily performance, while the more spirited 1.6-liter turbo engine (N Line) provides enhanced acceleration and responsiveness. Well-tuned suspension and precise steering make the Elantra comfortable on city streets and capable on the highway.

Interior & Comfort

Inside, the cabin focuses on driver comfort and functionality. Quality materials, a large touchscreen infotainment system, and intuitive controls create an enjoyable environment for both driver and passengers. Supportive seating, ample legroom, and a quiet ride enhance the overall comfort, making it suitable for daily commutes and road trips.
